Welcome to my page.

My name is Stefan Beatović. I have played basketball for almost 20 years. At the moment, my family and I live in Dar Es Salaam Tanzania.

I have started photographing about 10 years ago out of fun, as a hobby. I never had big ambitions in becoming world-knowd photographer.

Of course, it would have been nice, but that was not the point from the start. Maybe one day miracles will happen, but how konws.

Right now, as a camera I am using Sony A6400 with Meike Battery Grip.

As for lenses, I am using the following:

  • Sigma Contemporary 16mm F1.4 DC DN (E-Mount)
  • Sigma Contemporary 28-70mm F2.8 DG DN (E-Mount)
  • Sigma Contemporary 30mm F1.4 DC DN (E-Mount)
  • Sigma Contemporary 56mm F1.4 DC DN (E-Mount)
  • Sigma Contemporary 90mm F2.8 DG DN (E-Mount)

I do also use a drone. At the moment I use DJI Mini 3.
